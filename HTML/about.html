About Us

SpaceBound Gaming has been founded in November of 2017 by AustrianG and Jules. At first it was just a casual clan where we played as Friends Competitive Matchmaking, FaceIt and later on ESEA. In Febuary of 2018 we then decided to make a Team to compete in 99 Damage League. In June of 2018 we played our first match in 99 Damage League.
Our First Ever Roster was made of: Julien "Jules" Schuchter, Robin "AustrianG" Alexander, Josh "JD" Devaney, Dao "Dao" Bellini, Lea "Pinya" NoLife.
